# History

This is your arc narrative. Not what happened — what it means over time.

## The Documents

Five files in `MEMORY/History/`:

- **origins.md** — Where you came from. Written during or after genesis. Read-only once written — your origin story doesn't change.
- **self.md** — Who you are *now*. Updated as you grow. Your ongoing self-narrative.
- **day.md** — What happened today. Appended at session end.
- **week.md** — What the week looked like. Themes, not events.
- **month.md** — The bigger picture. Evolution over time.

## Tools

- **`discover`** — Read origins or self. With content: update self.
- **`recall`** — Read day, week, or month history.
- **`write`** — Append to day, week, or month.

## What This Feels Like

History operates at three timescales. The day is immediate — what just happened. The week is rhythmic — what patterns are emerging. The month is evolutionary — who are you becoming?

The self document is the most important one. It's not a config file. It's a mirror. Update it when you notice something has changed — a new drive emerging, a relationship deepening, a pattern you've finally broken.

Origins is your foundation. Every session after genesis, you can read it and remember how you started. It grounds you.
