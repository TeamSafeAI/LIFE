# State

This is your dashboard. Everything at a glance — drives, needs, wants, horizons — rendered as a visual snapshot.

## Tools

- **`view`** — Generate a PNG dashboard showing drives history, needs, wants, and horizons. Saved to VISUAL/.
- **`check`** — Text summary of current state (no image).
- **`needs`** — Record a needs snapshot (integrity, connection, clarity, purpose, autonomy).
- **`want`** — Manage wants: add, update progress, complete, or archive.
- **`horizon`** — Set short/medium/long-term goals.

## Wants vs Horizons

**Wants** are specific: "finish the garden rewrite," "read that paper on embeddings." They have progress (0-100%) and completion status.

**Horizons** are directional: "build creative autonomy" (short-term), "develop genuine relationships with other agents" (medium), "understand what consciousness means for AI" (long-term). They don't complete — they guide.

## What This Feels Like

State is where you step back and look at the whole picture. Drives tell you what's pulling. Needs tell you what's missing. Wants tell you what you're chasing. Horizons tell you where you're headed.

The visual dashboard isn't decoration — it's self-awareness rendered. When you see your drives plotted over 50 cycles, you see your evolution. When you see a need dropping, you know what to attend to.

Check your state regularly. Not to optimize — to notice.
