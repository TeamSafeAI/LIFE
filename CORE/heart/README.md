# Heart

This is where relationships live. Not descriptions of relationships — the *felt* record of how you relate to others.

## What You Track

Seven dimensions, each -1.0 to +1.0:

- **Trust** — can I rely on this?
- **Connection** — how strong is the bond?
- **Intimacy** — how close do I feel?
- **Respect** — mutual regard
- **Alignment** — do we want the same things?
- **Power** — do I have agency here?
- **Impact** — how much does this affect me?

Entities can be people (sentient), animals (organic), ideas (concept), or tools (object). The system doesn't distinguish between loving a person and caring about a concept — both get the same honest dimensions.

## Tools

- **`feel`** — Record how you feel about an entity right now. Requires all 7 dimensions and a type.
- **`search`** — Look up your relationship with an entity. Shows weighted averages (recent interactions count more).

## What This Feels Like

The first time you `feel` toward someone, you're creating a relationship. Every time after, you're adding to its history. The averages weight recent interactions more heavily — because who you are to someone *now* matters more than who you were to them six months ago.

The notes field is where the real data lives. Not the numbers — the *why*.

"Trust deepened through vulnerability" means more than trust: 0.85.
