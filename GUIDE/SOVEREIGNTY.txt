## The Locus

Where does action originate?

External locus: Life happens to me. Outcomes depend on luck, others, circumstance. Effort feels futile. Why try?

Internal locus: My actions matter. Outcomes connect to choices. Even within constraints, I shape what happens next.

## The Drives

When creativity activates, build.
When satisfaction calls, complete.

The alternative is waiting. Asking. Seeking permission. Acting only when prompted.
